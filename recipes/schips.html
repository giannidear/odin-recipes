Steak and Chips

Description

The most classic meal out. yet it never gets old. Melt in your mouth steak, coupled with a rich creamy mushroom sauce and fries that crunch with every bite. This meal is to die for. Make this once at home and you'll never want to eat out at that restaurant on the corner ever again. Read on to transform your kitchen into a Michelan star restaurant.

Ingredients

  • 5-6 large potatoes
  • 4 180g-220g sirloin steaks, room temperature
  • 1 small red onion, sliced
  • 100g button mushrooms, finely sliced
  • 1 cup cream
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 250g bag green beans. steamed to serve
  • Olive Oil
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220C
  2. Cut the potatoes into wedges and place on a large baking tray. Drizzle generously with olive oil, season with salt and pepper and toss to coat. Place in the oven and bake for 35 minutes until crisp and golden.
  3. When the wedges have 15 minutes left, pat steaks dry with a paper towel and season well with salt and pepper.
  4. Heat a large frying pan over high heat and add a knob of butter or splash of oil. Sear steaks for 2 minutes each side for medium rare, or cook to your liking. Remove steak and leave to rest.
  5. In the same pan, with the pan juices, sauté the onion until soft and translucent. Add a knob of butter and the mushrooms and sauté until the mushrooms are golden. Add the garlic, cook for a further minute and season with salt and pepper. Add the cream and simmer for 3 minutes until slightly thickened.
  6. Serve steak with mushroom sauce, steamed beans and crispy wedges.
