Self Saucing Chocolate Pudding

Description

Chocolate. Pudding. That sauces itself. Whether you're warming up on a cold winters day or you're comforting yourself from the outside world, this recipe will bring you unlimited amounts of joy. A rich chocolate sauce covering a melt in your mouth crust, top this beauty with a scoop of your favorite ice cream or cream and you'll be polishing off the entire pudding by yourself.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup / 125g Flour
  • 2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/4 Cup / 25g Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
  • 1/2 Cup / 100g White Sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 Cup / 125ml Milk
  • 1/2 Stick / 55g Butter
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract

Topping (Sauce)

  • 1 Cup / 200g Brown Sugar
  • 1/4 Cup / 25g Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
  • 1 1/4 Cup / 310ml Boiling Water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 160C / 325F
  2. In a medium sized mixing bowl add the dry ingredients, the flour, baking powder, sugar, cocoa powder and salt. Use a wooden spoon to combine the ingredients together and set to the side.
  3. Melt the butter in the microwave or in a small saucepan over low heat.
  4. Add the melted butter, milk, egg and vanilla to a small bowl. Whisk together with a fork until combined.
  5. Pour the wet ingredients into the bowl with the dry ingredients and gently folding the chocolate pudding batter together until combined.
  6. Pour the batter into a 9″ or 25cm round dish or a 20×30 or 9×13 dish.
  7. In a small bowl combine the brown sugar and cocoa powder together with a whisk.
  8. Gently sprinkle the sugar topping onto the chocolate pudding batter.
  9. Pour the boiling water over the back of a spoon over the chocolate pudding, being very careful not to burn yourself. This will stop the water disturbing the sugar topping below.
  10. Place the chocolate self saucing pudding into a 160C / 325F oven for 35-40 minutes or until bubbling at the edges. Let cool for 5-10 minutes before serving.
