Garlic Bread

Description

You already know that smell. You already know that crunch and you already know the flavor experience that bombs your mouth everytime you bite into one of the best foods ever created. Simplistic yet world renowned, garlic bread is a favorite among everone. Ready to make the best homemade delicacy that your kitchen has ever seen? Read on and enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1 450g loaf of Italian or French Bread
  • 1/2 cup (112g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large cloves garlic, smashed and minced
  • 1 heaping tablespoon chopped fresh parsely
  • 1/4 cup (25g) freshly grated Parmesan Cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Cut the loaf in half, horizontally. Mix the butter, garlic, and parsley together in a small bowl. Spread butter mixture over the the two bread halves.
  3. Place on a sturdy baking pan (one that can handle high temperatures, not a cookie sheet) and heat in a 350°F (175°C) oven for 10 minutes.
  4. Remove pan from oven. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over bread if you want. Return to oven on the highest rack.
  5. Broil on high heat for 2-3 minutes until the edges of the bread begin to toast and the cheese (if you are using cheese) bubbles. Watch very carefully while broiling. The bread can easily go from un-toasted to burnt.
  6. Remove from oven, let cool a minute. Remove from pan and use a bread knife to cut into 1-inch thick slices. Serve immediately.
